Some Frequently Asked Questions

1Does Pluto require admin or management permissions?

Pluto does not require admin or any administrative permissions. If the bot is not working properly, scroll down & watch our YouTube tutorial. If you want to admin pluto bot the bot will run easily in any channel.

2 Does Pluto Filters are free?

Yes, the filters of Pluto bot are completely free.

3Pluto bot is not working in my server. How to fix it?

Scroll up and watch the YouTube tutorial, it may be solution of your problem.

4 How do I play music using Pluto on Discord?

To play music with Pluto, use the command @Pluto play [song name/URL]. You need to mention bot to run every bot command. For example, @Pluto play Bass Song or @Pluto play https://youtu.be/uLMpSClgpQ8?si=ROpRhXBH4eWacDHU

5 How can I adjust the volume when playing music with Pluto?

To adjust the volume, use the command @Pluto volume [1-1000]. For instance, @Pluto volume 50 sets the volume to 50%.

6 What are the available music filters on Pluto?

Pluto offers various music filters like 3d, bass, nightcore, and more. To apply a filter, use the command @Pluto filter [filter name]. For example, @Pluto nightcore.

7 Can Pluto provide a list of available commands for reference?

Yes, you can use the command @Pluto help to get a list of available commands and functionalities categorized under Filters, Music, and Utilities.
